- **All**: View all available templates
- **Featured**: Curated top picks

- **Announcement**: Wedding announcements

## Rendering & Downloading

### What is Rendering?

Rendering creates a final video file with your wedding data permanently "burned in" to the video. This video can be:
- Downloaded and shared
- Posted on social media
- Sent to guests
- Used in invitations

### Starting a Render Job

1. Go to **"Render"** tab in the template modal
2. Click **"Start Rendering"**
3. Choose quality:
   - **HD (1920x1080)**: Best quality, larger file
   - **SD (1280x720)**: Good quality, smaller file

### Rendering Process

**Status Indicators:**
- **Queued**: Waiting in line
- **Processing**: Video is being rendered (1-3 minutes)
- **Completed**: Ready to download
- **Failed**: Error occurred (try again)

**Progress Bar:**
- Shows percentage complete
- Estimated time remaining
- Updates in real-time

### Downloading Your Video

1. Wait for status to show **"Completed"**
2. Click **"Download Video"** button
3. Video downloads to your device
4. File format: MP4 (compatible with all devices)

**Video Specs:**
- Format: MP4 (H.264)
- Resolution: Based on quality selected
- Duration: Same as original template
- File Size: 10-30MB (typical)

## FAQ

### Q: How many templates can I use?
**A:** You can apply one template at a time to your wedding. You can change it anytime.

### Q: Can I edit the template video itself?
**A:** No, the video is created by admins. You can customize text colors, fonts, and content.

### Q: What if my wedding data is missing?
**A:** Update your wedding details in your profile. The template will automatically update.

### Q: How long does rendering take?
**A:** Typically 1-3 minutes for a 30-second video. Longer videos take more time.

### Q: Can I render multiple times?
**A:** Yes! Render as many times as you need. Each render reflects your current customizations.

### Q: What if I don't like any template?
**A:** New templates are added regularly. Check back often, or contact support for custom requests.

### Q: Is there a cost for rendering?
**A:** Check your plan details. Some plans include unlimited renders, others may have limits.

### Q: Can I remove a template?
**A:** Yes, click **"Remove Template"** in your wedding dashboard to unassign the template.

### Q: What video format is downloaded?
**A:** MP4 format (H.264 codec), compatible with all devices and platforms.

### Q: Can I preview before rendering?
**A:** Yes! Always preview first. Rendering creates a static video file.

### Q: What if rendering fails?
**A:** Try again. If it fails repeatedly, contact support with your render job ID.

## Troubleshooting

### Problem: Video won't play
**Solution:** 
- Check internet connection
- Try a different browser
- Disable ad blockers
- Clear browser cache

### Problem: Rendering stuck at 0%
**Solution:**
- Wait 2-3 minutes (processing may take time)
- Refresh page and check status again
- If still stuck after 10 minutes, contact support

### Problem: Downloaded video has wrong data
**Solution:**
- Update wedding details
- Re-render video with updated data
- Check customizations were saved

### Problem: Can't download video
**Solution:**
- Check render status is "Completed"
- Try different browser
- Check download permissions
- Disable pop-up blockers
